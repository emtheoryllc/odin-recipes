Blended Baked Oats

Description

Who says eating your morning oatmeal has to be boring? Try these blended baked oats made with banana, egg, and almond milk. A really ripe banana adds sweatness. It reminds me of a muffin.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 large banana

  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
