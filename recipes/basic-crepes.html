Basic Crepes

Description

Reviewed by many users as "the best crepe receipe ever", Basic Crepes will knock your socks off.

Simple to make, and delicious to eat, you will have no problem diving in and making these for yourself or your family.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flower
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and the eggs. Gradually add in the milk and water, stirring to combine. Add the salt and butter; beat until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each crepe. Titl the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
  3. Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the bottom is light brown. Loosen with a spatula, turn and cook the other side. Serve hot.
